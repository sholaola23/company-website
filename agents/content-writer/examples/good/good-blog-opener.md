# Good Blog Post Openers

## Example 1: Specific stat hook
"150 orders a week. Zero admin time. That's what E'Manuel Bakery does now — but six months ago, the owner was spending every Thursday night tallying WhatsApp messages by hand."

**Why it works:** Specific number, contrast between before/after, makes you want to read the next line.

## Example 2: Direct question
"What happens when a customer fills in your contact form at 9pm on a Tuesday? If the answer is 'nothing until morning,' you're losing them to whoever responds first."

**Why it works:** Makes the reader check their own situation. Creates urgency without being pushy.

## Example 3: Counter-intuitive claim
"The biggest reason small businesses fail with AI isn't the technology — it's that they start with the wrong problem."

**Why it works:** Challenges assumptions, creates curiosity.

## Example 4: Concrete before/after
"Last month, a salon owner in Manchester spent 6 hours a week replying to the same 5 customer questions. This month, an AI assistant handles all of them — and she's booked 12 more appointments."

**Why it works:** Relatable scenario, specific numbers, clear outcome.
