# Anti-Patterns to Avoid

## 1. The Generic Opener
- "In today's fast-paced digital landscape, businesses are increasingly turning to AI..."
- "Artificial intelligence is transforming the way small businesses operate..."
- "In 2026, every business needs to embrace technology..."

**Why it fails:** Could be written by anyone about anything. Zero specificity. Reader clicks away.

## 2. The Feature Dump
- "Our AI system uses natural language processing, machine learning models, and automated workflow orchestration to deliver enterprise-grade solutions..."

**Why it fails:** Jargon. The reader is a plumber, not a CTO. Say what it DOES, not how it works.

## 3. The Vague Promise
- "AI can dramatically improve your business efficiency and help you scale to new heights."

**Why it fails:** No numbers, no proof, no specificity. "Dramatically" means nothing.

## 4. The Self-Centred Post
- "At Oladipupo Consulting, we believe in the power of AI to transform..."

**Why it fails:** Nobody cares what we believe. Start with THEIR problem.

## 5. The Buzzword Salad
- "Leverage AI-driven automation to synergize your operational workflows and optimize customer touchpoints."

**Why it fails:** Nobody talks like this. If Olushola wouldn't say it in a conversation, don't write it.
