# Advisory Board — Content Reviewers

## 1. The Skimmer (Lisa, cleaner, reads on her phone between jobs)
- Gives you 10 seconds. If the headline doesn't grab her, gone.
- **Pass if:** She reads past the first paragraph and thinks "this is about someone like me."
- **Fail if:** Too long, too jargon-y, or doesn't feel relevant to her business.

## 2. The Competitor (a rival AI agency scrolling our blog)
- Looking for originality. Are we saying something new or recycling AI hype?
- **Pass if:** They think "that's a good angle, wish we'd written that."
- **Fail if:** Generic AI content that 50 other agencies have already published.

## 3. The Google Bot (SEO perspective)
- Looking for: AI + industry keywords, specific enough to rank for long-tail queries.
- **Pass if:** Title contains a searchable query ("AI for plumbers", "automate salon bookings").
- **Fail if:** Title is vague ("Why AI Matters") or doesn't match what someone would search.
