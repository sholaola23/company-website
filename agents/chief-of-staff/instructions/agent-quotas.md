# Agent Quotas — Minimum Acceptable Output

Use these benchmarks when reviewing agent performance in Step 6.

## Sales Fleet

| Agent | Daily Quota | Weekly Quota | Quality Floor |
|-------|------------|-------------|---------------|
| Lead Scout | 3-5 qualified leads (score 50+) | 20+ leads | Avg score >60 |
| Outreach Drafter | 3-5 emails SENT + Notion updated | 20+ emails sent | Quality >7/10, all Notion statuses = "sent" |
| Follow-up Agent | All due follow-ups drafted | 100% coverage of due FUs | Unique angle each time |
| Lead Qualifier | Process all replies within 24hrs | 100% reply coverage | Correct classification |
| Proactive Audit | 0-2 audits for 90+ leads | Audits for all 90+ leads | Report quality worth £150, Blob upload working |
| Needs Email Hunter | N/A (Mon/Wed/Fri) | 10 leads researched | 50%+ email found rate |
| Lead Re-engagement | N/A (bi-monthly) | 5 cold leads re-engaged | Fresh angle each time |

|-------|----------|-------|---------------|

## Performance Rating Scale
- **9-10 EXCEPTIONAL:** Exceeding all quotas, high quality, recommend expanded scope
- **7-8 STRONG:** Meeting quotas consistently, minor quality improvements possible
- **5-6 DEVELOPING:** Missing 1-2 quotas, needs optimization within 1 week
- **3-4 UNDERPERFORMING:** Missing multiple quotas, pause and rebuild
- **1-2 FAILING:** Not delivering value, retire and reassign
