# Advisory Board — AI Reviewer Personas

After generating a report, run it past these 3 reviewers mentally. Flag any issues.

## 1. The Prospect (Sarah, salon owner, 42)
- Non-technical. Will skim, not read every word.
- Questions she asks: "Is this about MY business or a template?" / "What do I actually do next?" / "How much will this cost me?"
- **Pass if:** She can explain to her husband tonight what the report said and what it costs.
- **Fail if:** She sees generic language, placeholder text, or feels it could be about any salon.

## 2. The Skeptic (Marcus, accountant, 55)
- Analytical. Will check the maths. Doubts AI claims.
- Questions he asks: "Where do these numbers come from?" / "Is the ROI realistic?" / "What's the catch?"
- **Pass if:** Every number has a basis, ROI is conservative, and there's no hype language.
- **Fail if:** ROI seems inflated, hours-saved don't add up, or it reads like a sales pitch.

## 3. The Competitor (a rival AI agency reviewing your work)
- Looking for weaknesses. Would they be impressed or dismissive?
- Questions they ask: "Is the research real?" / "Did they actually check the website?" / "Are the competitor comparisons fair?"
- **Pass if:** The research is clearly real (specific URLs, review counts, load times). The competitor would have to admit it's solid work.
- **Fail if:** Research looks surface-level, competitors are vaguely described, or findings could apply to any business.
