# Prospect Email Template

Create as a Gmail DRAFT (not send). Olushola reviews and sends manually.

## To
[prospect's email from pipeline]

## Subject
Your Free AI Readiness Audit — [Business Name]

## Tone Rules

**If Response Context was provided:**
- Open by directly referencing what they said
- Make it feel like a personal reply, not a broadcast
- Example: "Thanks for getting back to me — you mentioned [their specific pain point], and that's exactly what I focused on in the audit."

**If no Response Context:**
- Open with the most striking finding from the report
- Use a specific number or competitor comparison

## Body Structure (HTML email, max 150 words)
1. Personal opener (reference their response OR lead with the killer finding)
2. 2-3 bullet points: most compelling findings from the report (real numbers)
3. **Big CTA button** linking to the hosted report:
```html
<a href="[report-url]" style="display:inline-block; background:#6366f1; color:white; padding:14px 28px; border-radius:8px; text-decoration:none; font-weight:700; font-size:15px;">View Your AI Readiness Report →</a>
```
4. Single soft CTA: "If anything resonates, reply here or WhatsApp me on +44 7469 347 654 to book a quick call."

