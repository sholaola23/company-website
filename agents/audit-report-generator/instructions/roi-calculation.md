# ROI Calculation Rules

## Hourly Rates by Region
- **UK (standard):** £15/hr
- **London:** £20/hr
- **US clients:** £25/hr

## Formulas
For each AI opportunity:
- Hours saved/week x hourly rate = weekly savings
- Monthly = weekly x 4.3
- Annual = monthly x 12
- Add revenue recovery estimate (missed bookings/leads captured)

## Revenue Recovery Estimates
- Missed bookings captured: estimate 1-3 extra bookings/week at average ticket price
- Lead response improvement: estimate % of currently-lost enquiries recaptured
- Be CONSERVATIVE — under-promise on savings

## First-Year ROI Calculation
- Total annual savings / (setup cost + 12 months retainer) x 100 = ROI %
- Payback period = setup cost / monthly savings (in months)
