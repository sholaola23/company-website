# Research Methodology

## 1a. Website Analysis (if they have one)
WebFetch their website and assess:
- Mobile responsiveness
- Load speed (fast / moderate / slow)
- Contact form presence and quality
- Online booking capability
- Auto-response on contact form
- SSL certificate (https?)
- Last updated feel (modern or dated?)
- Google visibility (WebSearch "[their service] [their city]" — top 10?)

## 1b. Online Presence Audit
WebSearch across:
- Google Business Profile (reviews, rating, response to reviews)
- Facebook page (active? followers? response to messages?)
- Directory listings (Yell, Checkatrade, Bark, Google Maps)

## 1c. Competitor Check
WebSearch "[their service] [their city]" and note:
- Top 3 competitors with specific data (review counts, features, websites)
- What competitors do better
- Gaps the prospect could exploit

## 1d. Response Time Assessment
- Contact form auto-respond?
- Chatbot or live chat?
- After-hours response mechanism?
