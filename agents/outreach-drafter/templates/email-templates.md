# Outreach Email Templates

## Template 1: Tested Your Response Time
**Use when:** Pain Points mention slow response, or they have a website but it's underperforming.

**Subject lines:**
- "I tested [Business Name]'s response time — here's what happened"
- "[Business Name] is losing enquiries after 6pm"
- "What happens when someone contacts [Business Name] at 9pm?"

**Body:**
```
Hi [Name],

[OPENING — choose ONE, never reuse across emails in a batch:]
- OPTION A (observation): "I tried contacting [Business Name] through your website last week — [what happened]."
- OPTION B (question): "What happens when a customer fills in your contact form at 9pm on a Tuesday? Right now — nothing until you check it the next morning."
- OPTION C (stat): "67% of customers go with the first business that responds. If someone contacts [Business Name] after hours, they're probably calling your competitor by morning."
- OPTION D (competitor angle): "I noticed [competitor] in [area] has an AI system responding to customers instantly. [Business Name] could be doing the same."

Here's the thing — [specific observation about their website/response gap]. That's money walking out the door.

I've built an AI-powered response system that reads what the customer actually asked and sends a helpful, personalised reply within 60 seconds — day or night. Not a chatbot with canned answers. An AI assistant that understands their enquiry.

[CASE STUDY — adapt for their industry, see ../_shared/case-studies.md]

Would you be open to seeing a quick 2-minute demo of how it'd work for [Business Name] specifically? I can put one together — no cost, no strings.

[SIGNATURE — see ../_shared/ctas.md]
```

- "The Google problem costing [Business Name] customers"

**Body:**
```
Hi [Name],

[OPENING — choose ONE:]
- OPTION A (Google test): "I just searched for '[their service] in [their city]' on Google. [Business Name] doesn't appear anywhere. Your competitors — who probably aren't half as good — are getting those customers instead."
- OPTION B (customer perspective): "If I needed a [their service] in [their city] right now and searched Google, I'd never find [Business Name]."
- OPTION C (compliment + gap): "Your reviews on Facebook are brilliant — [specific compliment]. But the only people seeing them are the ones who already know you exist."
- OPTION D (stat): "93% of local purchases start with a Google search. Without a website, [Business Name] is invisible to all of them."

Here's what I do: I build simple, AI-powered websites for local businesses. Not complicated 50-page sites — just a clean page that shows what you do, lets people contact you, and has an AI assistant that responds to enquiries instantly, even at midnight.

[CASE STUDY]

Would you like to see what an AI-powered online presence could look like for [Business Name]? I can mock something up — takes me a day, costs you nothing.

[SIGNATURE]
```

**Body:**
```

[OPENING — choose ONE:]

The fix isn't complicated. I add AI-powered features to existing websites — instant customer response, smart booking, better Google visibility. Not a full rebuild. Targeted upgrades that bring in more customers, starting in about a week.

[CASE STUDY]

[SIGNATURE]
```

## Template 5: Hiring Signal (Job Ad Trigger)
**Use when:** Lead is actively hiring for an ops, admin, coordinator, or customer service role — signals manual work overload.

**Subject lines:**
- "The role you're hiring for — AI could do 60% of it"
- "Saw [Business Name] is hiring a [role] — worth a chat first?"
- "Before you hire an ops coordinator at [Business Name]"

**Body:**
```
Hi [Name],

Noticed [Business Name] is hiring a [role title].

Based on how most [industry] businesses handle [process], there's a good chance 60–70% of what that role does daily could be automated — things like [specific example: chasing leads, updating records, sending follow-ups, scheduling].

I've built systems for [similar businesses] that cut that kind of manual work down from hours to minutes. Before you take on a salary, it might be worth a 20-minute call to see what's actually fixable.

Happy to do a free diagnostic — I'll map out exactly where the time is going and what's automatable. No cost, no pitch.

Worth a look?

[SIGNATURE — see ../_shared/ctas.md]
```

**Signal sources to watch:** LinkedIn job postings, Indeed, company websites with a /careers page. Look for: "Operations Coordinator", "Admin Assistant", "Customer Service", "Office Manager", "Bookings Manager", "Data Entry".
