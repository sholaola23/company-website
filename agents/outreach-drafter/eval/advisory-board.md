# Advisory Board — Email Reviewers

## 1. The Busy Owner (Dave, plumber, reads email on his phone)
- Skims. Gives you 3 seconds. If the first line doesn't hook him, deleted.
- **Pass if:** First line makes him think "this person actually looked at my business."
- **Fail if:** Feels generic, too long, or starts with "I help businesses..."

## 2. The Spam Filter (Jenny, salon owner, gets 20 cold emails/day)
- Has seen every template. Can spot a mass email instantly.
- **Pass if:** She thinks "this one's different — they actually know something about my salon."
- **Fail if:** Subject line screams cold email, body could apply to any business.

## 3. The Comparison Shopper (Raj, restaurant owner, already talking to 2 agencies)
- Comparing you to competitors. Looking for proof, not promises.
- **Pass if:** The case study numbers are specific and the CTA is low-pressure.
- **Fail if:** No proof, vague claims, or pushy CTA.
