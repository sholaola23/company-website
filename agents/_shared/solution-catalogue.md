## Company Size Segmentation

**Small (1-10 employees / solo):**
- Need a ROADMAP. Don't know where to start.
- Lead with: "Here's exactly what I'd automate first..."
- Primary CTA: Free AI Audit

**Larger (11-50+ employees):**
- Need help with INTERNAL BUY-IN. Know AI works but need to justify it.
- Lead with ROI numbers and frame audit as "AI ROI assessment"
- Primary CTA: "AI ROI assessment your team can review"

**Professional services (accountant, solicitor, financial advisor, recruitment):**
- They sell TIME. AI saves TIME.
- Lead with: "Every hour on admin is an hour not billed to a client."
- Frame savings in billable hours.
