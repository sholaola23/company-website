# CTA Variations

## Primary: Free AI Readiness Audit
- "Can I put together a free AI audit for [Business Name]? It'll show you exactly how many hours you could save."
- "I do free AI Readiness Audits for businesses like yours — shows the specific opportunities with ROI numbers. Want one?"
- "I'd love to run a quick AI audit on [Business Name] — no cost, no obligation. Just a report showing where AI could help."

## For High Scorers (80+)
- "I've already started putting together an AI audit for [Business Name] — want me to finish it and send it over?"

## Fallback (if audit feels too heavy)
- "Can I send a 2-minute demo?"
- "Does a 10-minute call this week work?"

## For Professional Services
- "I can put together an AI ROI assessment for [Business Name] — specific numbers your team can review."

## Framing Rules
- Always low-pressure: "No cost", "No strings", "No obligation"
- Pair with the AUDIT offer as the specific ask
- Always include opt-out: "If this isn't relevant, just let me know and I won't follow up."
