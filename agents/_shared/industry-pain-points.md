# Industry Pain Points & What to Sell
## Source: Luke Pierce (80+ companies, 4 years) — adapted for Oladipupo Consulting

**Rule: Never pitch "AI automation." Pitch the fix for THAT industry's specific bottleneck.**

---

## Tier 1 — Our Core ICP (active targets)

### 1. Home Services (HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical)
**Pain:** Missed calls = missed revenue. Phone rings, nobody answers, job goes to competitor.
**Sell:** AI phone answering that captures caller info, books appointments, routes urgent requests. No receptionist needed.
**Our proof:** Voice AI service page + Heritage HVAC prospect page
**Blog:** oladipupoconsulting.co.uk/blog/voice-ai-small-business

### 2. Medical & Dental Practices
**Pain:** No-shows and scheduling chaos. Staff spends hours calling patients to confirm, reschedule, fill gaps.
**Sell:** Automated appointment reminders, AI rescheduling, waitlist management that fills cancellations in minutes.
**Our proof:** Aspire Dental + Pearl Dental prospect pages
**Blog:** oladipupoconsulting.co.uk/blog/reduce-no-shows-ai-reminders

### 3. Accounting Firms
**Pain:** Client document collection is painful. Tax season = chasing 200+ clients for the same documents every year.
**Sell:** Automated document request workflows, status tracking per client, AI reminders that reduce back-and-forth by 80%.
**Our proof:** 3 accounting leads in pipeline (WK, P&L, Gardner Webb)
**Blog:** oladipupoconsulting.co.uk/blog/ai-for-uk-accountants

### 4. Salons / Barbers / Beauty
**Pain:** No-shows (15-20%), last-minute cancellations, manual rebooking, no review collection.
**Sell:** AI reminder systems — WhatsApp confirmations, automated rebooking for cancellations, review requests after every appointment.
**Our proof:** 10 Instagram DMs sent to local salons
**Blog:** oladipupoconsulting.co.uk/blog/ai-for-salons

### 5. Commercial Cleaning Companies
**Pain:** Scheduling and quality verification are manual. Supervisors drive between sites to confirm work was done.
**Sell:** Automated scheduling, GPS check-in/check-out, photo verification, client-facing dashboards.
**Blog:** oladipupoconsulting.co.uk/blog/ai-save-cleaning-companies-time

### 6. E-Commerce / D2C Brands
**Pain:** Customer support volume scales faster than team. Returns, order status, "where's my package" eat 60%+ of tickets.
**Sell:** AI chatbot that handles tier-1 tickets instantly, escalates the rest. Product recommendation engine. Post-purchase flows.
**Our proof:** UWA Hair Care chatbot + Range Beauty chatbot (both live demos)

### 7. Law Firms / Solicitors
**Pain:** Intake is a mess. Forms, voicemails, emails sit for days. Potential clients go elsewhere.
**Sell:** Intake-to-assignment system that captures every lead, qualifies them, routes to the right person with zero manual sorting.
**Our proof:** 4 solicitor emails sent in today's campaign

## Tier 2 — High-value, should expand into

### 8. Real Estate Brokerages
**Pain:** Lead follow-up dies after day 2. Agents juggle 40+ leads manually, most go cold.
**Sell:** Automated lead nurture sequences, AI follow-up, centralised pipeline that routes leads to the right agent.
**Match to our stack:** Perfect for vault scripts + Voice AI + n8n workflows

### 9. Insurance Agencies
**Pain:** Policy renewals fall through the cracks. Agents manage hundreds of clients on spreadsheets, miss renewal windows.
**Sell:** Automated renewal pipeline — flags upcoming expirations, sends reminders, queues outreach at the right time.
**Match to our stack:** n8n workflow + email sequences + dashboard

### 10. Property Management Companies
**Pain:** Maintenance requests are a nightmare. Tenants email, call, text. Nothing tracked. Vendors assigned late.
**Sell:** Request system that captures every submission, categorises by urgency, auto-assigns vendors, updates tenants.
**Match to our stack:** WhatsApp bot + n8n + Google Sheets dashboard (like E'Manuel)

### 11. Fitness Studios & Gyms
**Pain:** Member retention drops and nobody notices. No system tracks who stopped showing up.
**Sell:** Engagement tracking that flags inactive members, triggers re-engagement sequences. Automated class reminders + upsells.
**Match to our stack:** Email/SMS sequences + dashboard

### 12. Restaurants & Hospitality
**Pain:** Inventory management is manual guesswork. Over-ordering wastes money, under-ordering kills the menu.
**Sell:** AI-powered inventory forecasting based on historical sales, seasonal patterns, reservations.
**Match to our stack:** Already serving food sector (E'Manuel Bakery)

## Tier 3 — Future expansion (higher complexity)

### 13. Recruiting & Staffing Firms
**Pain:** Resume screening is a time pit. Manually reviewing hundreds of applications per role.
**Sell:** AI screening that parses resumes, scores candidates, surfaces top 10% automatically.

### 14. Marketing Agencies
**Pain:** Reporting takes 5-10 hours/week. Pulling data from platforms, formatting client reports.
**Sell:** Automated reporting dashboards with live data and client-ready summaries.

### 15. SaaS Companies
**Pain:** Churn signals go unnoticed. Usage drops, tickets spike, nobody connects the dots until cancellation.
**Sell:** Churn prediction system — monitors usage, flags at-risk accounts, triggers retention workflows.

### 16. Construction Companies
**Pain:** Project updates in text threads, email chains, someone's head. Nothing centralised.
**Sell:** Project hub tracking milestones, automating client updates, flagging delays.

### 17. Auto Dealerships
**Pain:** Follow-up after test drives is weak. Most leads get one call then nothing.
**Sell:** Multi-touch follow-up triggered by lead behaviour, AI lead scoring.

### 18. Financial Advisors
**Pain:** Client communication is inconsistent. Some get quarterly check-ins, others hear nothing for a year.
**Sell:** Automated client touchpoint system — scheduled reviews, market updates, no client goes dark.

### 19. Nonprofits
**Pain:** Donor management is chaos. Tracking donations, thank-yous, campaigns all manual.
**Sell:** Donor CRM with automated acknowledgment, recurring donation tracking, campaign dashboards.

### 20. Wholesale & Distribution
**Pain:** Order processing is slow, error-prone. Sales reps take orders via email/phone, manually enter into system.
**Sell:** Automated order capture from any channel, validates against inventory, pushes to fulfilment.

## How Agents Should Use This File

**Lead Scout:** When scoring leads, match the industry to this file. A dental practice with no online booking = high score (pain point confirmed).

**Outreach Drafter:** Lead with the SPECIFIC pain point for that industry. "Missed calls" for HVAC. "No-shows" for dental. "Document chasing" for accountants. NEVER pitch generic "AI automation."

**Content Writer:** Each blog post should target ONE industry's pain point. The title should name the industry. The content should describe the exact bottleneck.

**Strategy Agent:** Use Tier 2 industries for expansion planning. Prioritise by: revenue potential × ease of delivery × existing proof.

**Proactive Audit Agent:** When generating audit reports, reference the specific pain point for that industry from this file.
