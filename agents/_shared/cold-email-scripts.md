## Source
Adapted from "The Cold Email Scripts Vault" by James (scaledifylabs). Original had 20 scripts for agency/lead gen.
Rewritten for WorkCrew's AI automation offer targeting local service businesses.

## Universal Rules (MUST follow)
1. **5 lines max** (including signature) — wall of text = ignored
2. **One CTA only** — "reply yes" or one question. Never two asks.
3. **"Reply yes" beats "book a call"** — reduce friction to zero. Send Cal.com link AFTER they reply.
4. **Sound human** — no corporate jargon, write like you talk
5. **PS line gets read first** — use it for your best proof point

## A/B Testing Framework
1. Pick 3 scripts that fit the lead batch
2. Send 30+ emails with each (same lead quality, same time)
3. Measure reply rates after 7 days
4. Kill losers, scale winner
5. Test variations of the winner (subject line, PS line, CTA)

**What "good" looks like:**
| Reply Rate | Verdict |
|---|---|
| < 0.5% | Script or offer is broken |
| 0.5% - 1% | Decent, room to improve |
| 1% - 2% | Solid performer |
| 2%+ | Winner — scale this |
