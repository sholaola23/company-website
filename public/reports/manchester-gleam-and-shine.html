AI Opportunity Map
1. AI Lead Intake System
2.5 hrs saved/week
Current State
Potential customers visit your Facebook or find your number via Yell and call or send an email to request a quote. Responses take hours — sometimes overnight. Many prospects give up and book a competitor who responds immediately.
AI Solution
A simple branded landing page with an AI-powered enquiry form. When someone submits their details (name, address, cleaning type, preferred day), they instantly receive a personalised quote confirmation email within 60 seconds — automatically. You get a notification, they're already sold on responding speed.
2. Automated Online Booking & Scheduling
1.5 hrs saved/week
Current State
All bookings are managed manually via phone and email. Off-hours enquiries (evenings, weekends) go unanswered until the next working day. Customers who want to book at 9pm on a Sunday — your busiest competitor signup window — are being lost.
AI Solution
A self-serve booking calendar lets customers pick service type, date, and time. Confirmation emails and 24-hour reminder texts send automatically. No admin required. Works while you sleep. A client I work with — a local service business — freed up 5 hours of weekly admin by switching to this model.
3. Quote Follow-up Automation
1 hr saved/week
Current State
When someone asks for a quote and doesn't book, there's no structured follow-up process. These warm leads — people who were interested enough to make contact — simply go cold and are never recovered.
AI Solution
Automated 2-step follow-up sequence: Day 2, a friendly "just checking in" message. Day 5, a gentle nudge with a first-clean incentive. This alone typically converts 10–20% of previously lost quotes into paid bookings.
4. Google Review Generation
0.5 hrs saved/week
Current State
Manchester Gleam & Shine has 5 Google reviews after 7 years of trading. Competitors have 276 to 664 reviews. When a potential customer searches "cleaning service Manchester," they will not find you above businesses with 50x more social proof — no matter how good your work is.
AI Solution
After every clean, an automated text or email asks the customer to leave a Google review with a direct link. Simple, low-friction, and proven to generate 8–12x more reviews than asking verbally. Claim and optimise your Google Business Profile at the same time. Within 90 days, you can realistically reach 40–60 reviews.
