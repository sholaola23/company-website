## POST 7: Quick Tip — Response Time
**Post date:** Tuesday 1 April, 08:00 UK

The #1 reason small businesses lose leads:

Slow response time.

Here's a stat that should terrify you:

Leads contacted within 5 minutes are 9x more likely to convert.

Nine times.

Now think about your business.

When someone fills in your contact form — how long until they hear back?

An hour?
A day?
"When I get round to it"?

Meanwhile they've messaged 3 competitors.

And the first one to reply wins.

Every time.

Here's the thing — you don't need AI to fix this.

Start with an auto-reply.

"Thanks for getting in touch. We've received your message and will respond within [X hours]."

That's it. That one automated message buys you time and stops the lead from bouncing.

Then level up:

→ Auto-qualify the lead (are they in your area? Do they need what you sell?)
→ Auto-book a call if they're a good fit
→ Auto-follow up if they go quiet

This is automation 101.

And it's the single biggest ROI improvement most small businesses can make.
